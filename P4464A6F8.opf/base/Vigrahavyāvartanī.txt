vigrahavyāvartanīsarveṣāṃ bhāvānāṃ sarvatra na vidyate svabhāvaścet|tvadvacanamasvabhāvaṃ na
nivartayituṃ svabhāvamalam

atha sasvabhāvametadvākyaṃ śrutvā hatā pratijñā te|vaiṣamikatvaṃ tasmin viśeṣahetuśca vaktavyaḥ

mā śabdavadityetat syātte buddhirna caitadupapannam|śabdenātra satā bhaviṣyato vāraṇaṃ tasya

pratiṣedhaḥ pratiṣeddhyo'pyevamiti mataṃ bhavet tadasadeva|evaṃ tava pratijñā lakṣaṇato duṣyate
na mama

pratyakṣeṇa hi tāvan yadyupalabhya vinivartayasi bhāvān|tannāsti pratyakṣaṃ bhāvā yenopalabhyante

anumānaṃ pratyuktaṃ pratyakṣeṇāgamopamāne ca|anumānāgamasādhyā ye'rthā dṛṣṭāntasādhyāśca

kuśalānāṃ dharmāṇāṃ dharmāvasthāvidaśca manyante|kuśalaṃ janasvabhāvaṃ śeṣeṣvapyeṣa viniyogaḥ

nairyāṇikasvabhāvo dharmo nairyāṇikāśca ye teṣām|dharmāvasthoktānāmeva ca nairyāṇikādīnām

yadi ca na bhavet svabhāvo dharmāṇāṃ niḥsvabhāva ityevam|nāmāpi bhavennaivaṃ nāmāpi nirvastukaṃ
nāsti

atha vidyate svabhāvaḥ sa ca dharmāṇāṃ na vidyate tasmāt|dharmairvinā svabhāvaḥ sa yasyāsti
tad yuktamupadeṣṭum

sata eva pratiṣedho nāsti ghaṭo geha ityayaṃ yasmāt|dṛṣṭaḥ pratiṣedho'yaṃ sataḥ svabhāvasya
te tasmāt

atha nāsti sa svabhāvaḥ kiṃ nu pratividhyate tvayānena|vacanenarte vacanāt pratiṣedhaḥ sidhyate
hyasataḥ

bālānāmiva mithyā mṛgatṛṣṇāyāṃ yathā jalagrāhaḥ|evaṃ mithyāgrāhaḥ syātte pratiṣidhyato hyasataḥ

nanvevaṃ satyasti grāho grāhyaṃ ca tagdṛhītaṃ ca|pratiṣedhaḥ pratiṣedhyaṃ pratiṣeddhā ceti ṣaṭkaṃ
tat

atha naivāsti grāho na ca grāhyaṃ na ca grahītāraḥ|pratiṣedhaḥ pratiṣedhyaṃ pratiṣeddhāro'sya
tu na santi

pratiṣedhaḥ pratiṣedhyaṃ pratiṣeddhāraśca yadyuta na santi|siddhā hi sarvabhāvā yeṣāmevaṃ svabhāvaśca

hetostato na siddhirnaiḥsvābhāvyāt kuto hi te hetuḥ|nirhetukasya siddhirna copapannāsya te'rthasya

yadi cāhetoḥ siddhiḥ svabhāvavinivartanasya te bhavati|svābhāvyasyāstitvaṃ mamāpi nirhetukaṃ
siddham

atha hetorastitvaṃ bhāvanaiḥsvābhāvyamityanupapannam|loke naiḥsvābhāvyānna hi kaścana vidyate
bhāvaḥ

pūrvaṃ cet pratiṣedhaḥ paścāt pratiṣedhyamiti ca nopapannam|paścādanupapanno yugapacca yataḥ
svabhāvo'san

hetupratyayasāmagryāṃ pṛthagbhāve'pi madvaco na yadi|nanu śūnyatvaṃ siddhaṃ bhāvānāmasvabhāvatvāt

yaśca pratītya bhāvo bhāvānāṃ śūnyateti sā hyuktā|yaśca pratītya bhāvo bhavati hi tasyāsvabhāvatvam

nirmitako nirmitakaṃ māyāpuruṣaḥ svamāyayā sṛṣṭam|pratisedhayase yadvat pratiṣedho'yaṃ tathaiva
syāt

na svābhāvikametad vākyaṃ tasmānna vādahānirme|nāsti ca vaiṣamikatvaṃ viśeṣahetuśca na nigadyaḥ

mā śabdavaditi nāyaṃ dṛṣṭānto yastvayā mamārabdhaḥ|śabdena hi tacca śabdasya vāraṇaṃ naiva me
vacaḥ

naiḥsvābhāvyānāṃ cennaiḥsvābhāvyena vāraṇaṃ yadi hi|naiḥsvābhāvyanivṛttau svābhāvyaṃ hi prasiddhaṃ
syāt

athavā nirmitakāyāṃ yathā striyāṃ striyamityasaṃgrāham|nirmitakaḥ pratihanyāt kasyacidevaṃ bhavedetat

athavā sādhyasamo'yaṃ heturna hi vidyate dhvaneḥ sattā|saṃvyavahāraṃ ca vayaṃ nānabhyupagamya
kathayāmaḥ

yadi kācana pratijñā tatra syādeṣa me bhaveddoṣaḥ|nāsti ca mama pratijñā tasmānnaivāsti me doṣaḥ

yadi kiṃcidupalabheyaṃ pravartayeyaṃ nivartayeyaṃ vā|pratyakṣādibhirarthaistadabhāvānme'nupālambhaḥ

yadi ca pramāṇatasteṣāṃ teṣāṃ prasiddhirarthānām|teṣāṃ punaḥ prasiddhiṃ brūhi kathaṃ te pramāṇānām

anyairyadi pramāṇaiḥ pramāṇasiddhirbhavatyanavasthā|nādeḥ siddhistatrāsti naiva madhyasya nāntasya

teṣāmatha pramāṇairvinā prasiddhirvihīyate vādaḥ|vaiṣamikatvaṃ tasmin viśeṣahetuśca vaktavyaḥ

viṣamopanyāso'yaṃ na hyātmānaṃ prakāśayatyagniḥ|na hi tasyānupalabdhirdṛṣṭā tamasīva kumbhasya

yadi svātmānamayaṃ tvadvacanena prakāśayatyagniḥ|paramiva na tvātmānaṃ paridhakṣyatyapi hutāśaḥ

yadi ca svaparātmānau tvadvacanena prakāśayatyagniḥ|pracchādayiṣyati tamaḥ svaparātmānau hutāśa
iva

nāsti tamaśca jvalane yatra ca tiṣṭhati sadātmani jvalanaḥ|kurute kathaṃ prakāśaṃ sa hi prakāśo'ndhakāravadhaḥ

utpadyamāna eva prakāśayatyagnirityasadvādaḥ|utpadyamāna eva prāpnoti tamo na hi hutāśaḥ

aprāpto'pi jvalano yadi vā punarandhakāramupahanyāt|sarveṣu lokadhātuṣu tamo'yamihasaṃsthita
upahanyāt

yadi ca svataḥpramāṇasiddhiranapekṣya te prameyāṇi|bhavati pramāṇasiddhirna parāpekṣā hi siddhiriti

anapekṣya hi prameyānarthān yadi te pramāṇasiddhiḥ|bhavati na bhavati kasyacidevamimāni pramāṇāni

atha matamapekṣya siddhisteṣāmityatra ko doṣaḥ|siddhasya sādhanaṃ syānnāsiddho'pekṣate hyanyat

sidhyanti hi prameyāṇyapekṣya yadi sarvathā pramāṇāni|bhavati prameyasiddhiranapekṣyaiva pramāṇāni

yadi ca prameyasiddhiranapekṣyaiva bhavati pramāṇāni|kiṃ te pramāṇasiddhyā tāni yadarthaṃ prasiddhaṃ
tat

atha tu pramāṇasiddhirbhavatyapekṣyaiva te prameyāṇi|vyatyaya evaṃ sati te dhruvaṃ pramāṇaprameyāṇām

atha tai pramāṇasiddhyā prameyasiddhiḥ prameyasiddhyā cābhavati pramāṇasiddhirnāstyubhayasyāpi
te siddhiḥ

sidhyanti hi pramāṇairyadi prameyāṇi tāni taireva|sādhyāni ca prameyaistāni kathaṃ sādhayiṣyanti

sidhyanti ca prameyairyadi pramāṇāni tāni taireva|sādhyāni ca prameyaistāni kathaṃ sādhayiṣyanti

pitrā yadyutpādyaḥ putro yadi tena caiva putreṇa|utpādyaḥ sa yadi pitā vada tatrotpādayati kaḥ
kam

kaśca pitā kaḥ putrastatra tvaṃ brūhi tāvubhāvapi ca|pitāputralakṣaṇadharau yato naḥ putrasaṃdehaḥ

naiva svataḥprasiddhirna parasparataḥ pramāṇairvā|bhavati na ca prameyairna cāpyakasmāt pramāṇānām

kuśalānāṃ dharmāṇāṃ dharmāvasthāvidho brūvate yat|kuśalasvabhāvamevaṃ pravibhāgenābhidheyaḥ
syāt

yadi ca pratītya kuśalaḥ svabhāva utpadyate sa kuśalānām|dharmāṇāṃ parabhāvaḥ svabhāva evaṃ
kathaṃ bhavati

atha na pratītya kiṃcit svabhāva utpadyate sa kuśalānām|dharmāṇāmevaṃ syāda vāso na brahmacaryasya

nādharmo dharmo vā saṃvyavahārāśca laukikā na syuḥ|nityāśca sarvabhāvāḥ syurnityatvādahetumataḥ

eṣa cākuśaleṣvavyākṛteṣu nairyāṇādiṣu ca doṣaḥ|tasmāt sarvaṃ saṃskṛtamasaṃskṛtaṃ te bhavatyevam

yaḥ sadbhūtaṃ nāma brūyāt sa svabhāva ityevam|bhavatā prativaktavyo nāma brūmaśca na vyaṃ sat

nāmāsaditi ca yadidaṃ tatkiṃ nu sato bhavatyutāsataḥ|yadi hi sato yadyasato dvidhāpi te hīyate
vādaḥ

sarveṣāṃ bhāvānāṃ śūnyatvaṃ copapāditaṃ pūrvam|sa upālambhastasmād bhavatyayaṃ ca pratijñāyāḥ

atha vidyate svabhāvaḥ sa ca dharmāṇāṃ na vidyata iti|idamāśaṅkitaṃ yaduktaṃ bhavatyanāśaṅkitaṃ
tacca

sata eva pratiṣedho yadi śūnyatvaṃ nanvapratiṣiddhamidam|pratiṣedhayate hi bhavān bhāvānāṃ niḥsvabhāvatvam

pratiṣedhayase'tha tvaṃ śūnyatvaṃ tacca nāsti śūnyatvam|pratiṣedhaḥ sata iti te nanvevaṃ hīyate
vādaḥ

pratiṣedhayāmi nāhaṃ kiṃcit pratiṣedhyamasti na ca kiṃcit|tasmāt pratiṣedhayasītyadhilaya eva
tvayā kriyate

yaccāhaṃ te vacanādasataḥ pratiṣedhavacanasiddhiriti|atra jñāpayate vāgasaditi tanna pratinihanti

mṛgatṛṣṇādṛṣṭānte yaḥ punaruktaṃ tvayā mahāṃścaryaḥ|tatrāpi nirṇayaṃ śṛṇu yathā sa dṛṣṭānta
upapannaḥ

sa yadi svabhāvataḥ syāt bhāvo na syāt pratītyasamudbhūtaḥ|yaśca pratītya bhavati grāho nanu
śūnyatā saiva

yadi ca svabhāvataḥ syād grāhaḥ kastaṃ nivartayed grāhyam|śeṣeṣvapyeṣa vidhistasmād doṣo'nupālambhaḥ

etena hetvabhāvaḥ pratyuktaḥ pūrvameva sa samatvāt|mṛgatṛṣṇādṛṣṭāntavyāvṛttividhau ya uktaḥ
prāk

yastraikālye hetuḥ pratyuktaḥ pūrvameva sa samatvāt|traikālyapratihetuśca śūnyatāvādināṃ prāptaḥ

prabhavati ca śūnyateyaṃ yasya prabhavanti tasya sarvārthāḥ|prabhavati na tasya kiṃ na bhavati
śūnyatā yasyeti

yaḥ śūnyatāṃ pratītyasamutpādaṃ madhyamāṃ pratipadamanekārthām|nijagāda praṇamāmi tamapratimasaṃbuddham

iti||
